Platform Features

Everything. One platform.

We've built what BomaHut, AppFolio and Buildium combined still can't offer for the Kenyan market.

💳

Smart Payment Engine

Real-time M-Pesa Paybill integration. Bank transfers from Equity, KCB, Co-op. Automatic payment recording the second money lands. KES 20/day penalty auto-applied after 10th. SMS receipt within 5 seconds of payment.

M-Pesa Daraja API Auto-Receipts Penalty Engine WhatsApp Receipts
🔧

Repair & Technician Network

6 categories. Vetted local technicians. Real-time job assignment. Live status tracking from "submitted" to "resolved." Tenant can call tech directly.

🤝

Tenant Screening

CRB credit check via Metropol. ID verification via IPRS. Digital application forms. Online lease signing. Full paperless move-in workflow.

📊

AI Analytics & Rent Prediction

Machine learning flags tenants likely to default before they do. Predicts vacancy. Suggests optimal rent pricing based on market data. KRA-ready annual financial statements generated automatically.

💧

Utility Auto-Billing

Water, electricity, garbage — enter meter readings and bills split automatically per unit. No more arithmetic disputes.

🗑️

Garbage Alerts

Automated SMS every Tuesday 6PM and Wednesday 5:45AM. Live countdown timer in tenant app. Bin-out reminders never missed.

⭐

Rent Score™

Tenants build a verified rental credit score. Share it when applying for new homes, loans, or jobs. Kenya's first rental credit system.

🏘️

Digital Community Board

Private resident social network per apartment. Share resources, post events, emergency alerts, lost & found, neighbour requests. Curated monthly events calendar managed by landlord.

📱

USSD + WhatsApp + App

Smartphone app, mobile browser, WhatsApp bot, and USSD *384# for basic phones. Every Kenyan can use NyumbaLink regardless of device or data access.
